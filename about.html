About IWB

Our Journey

IWB is a dynamic company based in Lesotho, founded under the vision and leadership of Co-Founder Kenneth. Starting in 2024 with an initial capital of M100,000, our journey began with a clear mission: to revolutionize electronic waste management.

As we sought to expand our reach, Shadrack joined as a second CEO, bringing invaluable expertise and partnership. Together, we've steered IWB towards becoming a pioneer in electronic recycling, focusing on the sustainable repurposing of RAM, Hard Drives, and Motherboard components.

By 2025, IWB had not only established a strong foothold in the Lesotho market but also began attracting attention from investors, partners, and clients across the Southern Region of Africa and beyond.

Our Timeline

  • 2024: IWB is founded by Kenneth with a capital of M100,000, focusing on electronic recycling.
  • 2024: Shadrack joins as a second CEO, strengthening the company's leadership.
  • 2025: IWB establishes itself as a pioneer in electronic recycling in the Southern Region of Africa, attracting international interest.
  • Present: IWB continues to innovate and expand, focusing on developing cutting-edge software tools for data recovery and destruction.

Our Mission

Our mandate extends beyond just recycling; we are committed to developing innovative solutions that address the growing challenges of data management and security. As part of this commitment, we are excited to introduce four new software tools designed for advanced data recovery and destruction.
